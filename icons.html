Guidelines

We maintain a custom icon set with a consistent style that is simple, airy, and legible, serving to compliment a visual aesthetic without detracting from usability. We heavily user-test our icons to ensure that they are interpreted correctly and perceived as consistent with the brand. As our user demographic is often not tech-savvy, any icons that are not easily recognized must also be accompanied with a text label.
